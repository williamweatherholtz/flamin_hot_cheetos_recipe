Flamin Hot Cheetos Chicken Chilli


16oz. can creamed corn
16oz. can kidney beans (drained)
16oz. can pinto beans (drained)
10oz. can Rotel tomatoes
28oz. can diced tomatoes

1 (1oz.) ranch mix packet
1 (1.25oz.) taco seasoning packet

1 Tbl serrano chili powder
1 tsp cayenne powder
1 tsp MSG
3 Cups chopped cooked/grilled chicken thighs

1/4 Cup lime juice

Combine & serve with sour cream, cheese, & tortilla chips.
